Data Visualization Project

Interactive climate data explorer

Year

2023

Client

Environmental Research Institute

Role

UX/UI Design, Data Visualization, Front-end Development

This interactive climate data explorer visualizes complex environmental datasets for researchers and the general public. The project aimed to make climate science more accessible by transforming dense numerical data into intuitive, interactive visualizations that reveal patterns and trends in global temperature changes, carbon emissions, and sea level rise.

I worked closely with climate scientists to understand the data and create visualizations that would accurately represent complex scientific information while remaining accessible to non-experts. The interface allows users to explore different timeframes, geographic regions, and environmental variables through a series of interconnected, responsive charts and maps.

The project was built using D3.js for data visualization, with a React front-end and Node.js back-end. Special attention was paid to accessibility features, ensuring that all visualizations include alternative text descriptions and keyboard navigation options.
